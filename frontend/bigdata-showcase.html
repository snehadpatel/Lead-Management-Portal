Why Parquet?

Columnar format: Only reads columns needed for query — 10-100x faster than CSV for analytical workloads.

Compression: Snappy encoding reduces storage by ~75% while maintaining fast read speeds.

Schema evolution: Supports schema validation and type safety that CSV lacks.
